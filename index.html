CFA Prep  ·  Formula of the Day
Today's Formula — DuPont ROE Decomposition (3-Factor)
ROE = (Net Income ÷ Revenue) × (Revenue ÷ Total Assets) × (Total Assets ÷ Equity)
= Net Profit Margin × Asset Turnover × Financial Leverage (Equity Multiplier)
Exam tip: Higher leverage boosts ROE mechanically — always check if a high-ROE firm is high-quality (margin/turnover driven) or just highly levered before drawing conclusions.
