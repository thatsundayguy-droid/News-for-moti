CFA Prep  ·  Formula of the Day
Today's Formula — Free Cash Flow to Equity (FCFE)
FCFE = NI + NCC − FCInv − WCInv + Net Borrowing
NI = net income  |  NCC = non-cash charges  |  FCInv = fixed capital investment
WCInv = increase in working capital  |  Net Borrowing = new debt issued − debt repaid
Exam tip: FCFE is discounted at the cost of equity, not WACC — use it to value equity directly, skipping the EV-to-equity bridge.
