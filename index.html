CFA Prep  ·  Formula of the Day
Today's Formula — Free Cash Flow to Firm (FCFF)
FCFF = NI + NCC + Int(1−t) − FCInv − WCInv
NI = net income  |  NCC = non-cash charges (e.g. depreciation)  |  Int(1−t) = after-tax interest expense
FCInv = fixed capital investment (CapEx)  |  WCInv = increase in working capital
Exam tip: FCFF is discounted at WACC, not cost of equity — pairing FCFF with cost of equity is a common L1 error.
