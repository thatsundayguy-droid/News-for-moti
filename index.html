CFA Prep  ·  Formula of the Day
Today's Formula — Net Interest Margin (NIM)
NIM = (Interest Income − Interest Expense) ÷ Average Earning Assets
Interest Income = interest earned on loans/investments  |  Interest Expense = interest paid on deposits/borrowings
Average Earning Assets = (Beginning + Ending Earning Assets) ÷ 2
Exam tip: NIM is the core profitability metric for banks, NBFCs and HFCs — rising cost of funds compresses NIM even if loan growth stays strong.
