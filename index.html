CFA Prep  ·  Formula of the Day
Today's Formula — Time Value of Money: Annuity PV
PV = PMT × [1 − (1 + r)−n] ÷ r
PMT = periodic payment  |  r = periodic discount rate  |  n = number of periods
Perpetuity: PV = PMT ÷ r  |  Growing perpetuity (Gordon Growth): PV = D⊂1; ÷ (r − g)
Exam tip: Annuity due PV = Ordinary annuity PV × (1 + r). Semi-annual bonds: use r/2 and 2n — not annual r.
