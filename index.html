CFA Prep  ·  Formula of the Day
Today's Formula — Bond Duration (Modified)
Modified Duration = Macaulay Duration ÷ (1 + YTM/m)
Macaulay Duration = weighted average time to receive cash flows  |  YTM = yield to maturity  |  m = compounding periods per year
Price change ≈ −Modified Duration × ΔYTM × Price
Exam tip: Price and yield move in opposite directions — so the negative sign is essential. If Modified Duration = 5 and yield rises 100 bps, price falls approximately 5%. Flipping this sign is a common exam error.
