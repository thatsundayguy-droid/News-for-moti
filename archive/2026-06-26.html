CFA Prep  ·  Formula of the Day
Today's Formula — Return on Equity (Single-Factor)
ROE = Net Income ÷ Average Shareholders' Equity
Net Income = bottom-line profit for the period  |  Average Equity = (Beginning Equity + Ending Equity) ÷ 2
Exam tip: Always use average equity, not ending equity — using ending equity overstates ROE when equity has grown during the period.
