CFA Prep  ·  Formula of the Day
Today's Formula — Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC)
WACC = (E÷V) × Re + (D÷V) × Rd × (1−t)
E = market value of equity  |  D = market value of debt  |  V = E + D
Re = cost of equity (CAPM)  |  Rd = pre-tax cost of debt  |  t = marginal tax rate
Exam tip: Always use market-value weights, never book values — using book value of equity is one of the most common L1 errors.
