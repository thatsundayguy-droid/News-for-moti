CFA Prep  ·  Formula of the Day
Today's Formula — Capital Adequacy Ratio (CRAR)
CRAR = (Tier 1 Capital + Tier 2 Capital) ÷ Risk-Weighted Assets
Tier 1 = core capital (equity + disclosed reserves)  |  Tier 2 = supplementary capital (subordinated debt, revaluation reserves)
RWA = assets weighted by credit risk
Exam tip: RBI mandates minimum 15% CRAR for NBFCs (10% Tier 1) — a bank or NBFC below this threshold faces regulatory restrictions on growth and dividends.
